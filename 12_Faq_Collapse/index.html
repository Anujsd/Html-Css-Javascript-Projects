What are the Official Languages of India?

The official language of the Republic of India is Hindi with English as a secondary official language

When is best time to travel to india?

It really depends on your destination, activities and season of travel. In much of Northern and Central India, the favored time to visit is from October to March, when the weather is most mild. In Southern and Eastern India, it is contingent on the monsoon season.
